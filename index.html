Notes

  • These BfA, non-elite rares give level 61 items at level 20, can only be looted once, can proc a socket and/or a tertiary stat, but not epic, and are always up (with a short respawn timer).
  • At the same time as travelling around the four BfA zones, you can loot chests for a level 61 helm from the quest The Great Sea Scrolls. Also get the 28 slot bag Goat's Tote from Totes (/way #895 46.8 20.6 Totes).
  • Hay Covered Chest in Tiragarde Sound, which rewards Garyl's Riding Blanket, starts with Guardian of the Spring. Horde players might want to skip this one!
  • Similarly, "hardcore" players might want to avoid The Caterer in Drustvar, as well as the nearby flight master, as an elite Spectral Guardian can appear and one-shot you. I'm not sure if this applies to Alliance.
  • Horde can travel to Tiragarde Sound by hopping on to the side of the Relentless from Stormwind Harbour; run up from Grom'gol. Its the boat that docks and departs heading south, from the most southerly dock (/way Stormwind City 22.5 55.5). Set your heathstone to Castaway point (/way #895 85.2 80.4 Friendly Pete) if you want to return easily.
  • Alliance can travel to Zuldazar on The Golden Skipper from the Echo Isles; run from Ratchet (/way Echo Isles 71 38).
