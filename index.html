Notes

These BfA rares give level 61 items at level 20, can only be looted once, can proc a socket and/or a tertiary stat, but not epic, and are always up (with a short respawn timer).

At the same time as travelling around the four BfA zones, you can loot chests for a level 61 helm from the quest The Great Sea Scrolls.

Hay Covered Chest in Tiragarde Sound, which rewards Garyl's Riding Blanket, starts with Guardian of the Spring. Horde players might want to skip this one!

Horde can travel to Tiragarde Sound by hopping on to the side of the Relentless from Stormwind Harbour; run up from Grom'gol. Its the boat that docks and departs heading south, from the most southerly dock (/way Stormwind City 22.5 55.5).
Alliance can travel to Zuldazar on The Golden Skipper from the Echo Isles; run from Ratchet (/way Echo Isles 71 38).
